College advisors initiate one-on-one work with juniors in mid-November. The College Advising Office encourages students and their parents to start touring campuses in the summer between their sophomore and junior years to begin to get a sense of what sort of college may seem like a good fit based on location, size, academic focus, and selectivity. A more comprehensive list of college suggestions will be created by the college advisor and the student in the winter of their junior year. Most juniors will take either the ACT or the SAT during the spring and AP exams in the first two weeks of May. Most students will take the ACT /SAT twice. We strongly encourage juniors to ask two teachers for recommendation letters for college application before the school closes for summer. Colleges generally expect to hear from two academic teachers, ideally in different subject areas: often one math/science teacher and one humanities teacher.
