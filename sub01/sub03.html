Grand Tour and its link to NCS

A voyage to becoming an elite!
The most essential course of elite education, giving birth to Europe’s brightest intellectuals like Adam Smith, Voltaire, Goethe.
Grand Tour, the world’s first ‘educational traveling’, made its debut in 17th century England, spreading towards all of Europe in the 18th century.
Young lords and aristocrats would travel all around the European continent including France and Italy, for 2~3 years, learning foreign languages,
diplomacy, art, manners, and developing fine taste.
Through borderless human interactions and spread of arts and ideologies, the Grand Tour laid the foundations for modern Europe.

The Next Challenge School has its roots in this very same foundation. In fact, NCS takes a step further, transforming you into a global talent, through
diverse experiences in various fields such as culture and art, that you will gain while exploring across the 6 continents and the 5 oceans.

One distinctive feature of the tour was that the young nobles were accompanied by private tutors to cultivate their academics, art, and culture.
Similarly, NCS also has faculty and staff accompanying students, who are responsible for their education. If you wish to become an innovative
leader in this rapidly changing era of globalization, join NCS on its trip around the world.
