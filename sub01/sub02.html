Mission, Vision,
and Values
Those who are wise will shine like the brightness of theheavens, and those who
lead many to righteousness, like the stars for ever and ever. ~ Daniel 12:3
  • Mission
  • Next Challenge School is a community of students, parents, and Christian educators, that is dedicated to the service of others, working together to inspire a passion for innovative-learning and pursue academic excellence alongside Christian values.
  • Vision
  • NCS graduates are innovative leaders who are creative, open to challenges, collaborative, global, and faithful. Eventually leading the world as servant-minded leaders.
Value
Expand
Expand your perspective.
Global education is not limited to the walls of a classroom.
All things in the world can be learning targets.
Learn
Learn to explore, research, document the world with wisdom and compassion, whilst living with
other students and teachers in different countries each term.
Motivate
Motivate students by providing them with an environment where students
can learn about themselves and develop whilst learning and understanding the world.
Design
Design curriculums so that students can exert cultural, historical, environmental, socioeconomic,
and overall positive influence throughout the surrounding world by tapping into their potential.
Contribute
Contribute a small but meaningful piece to making the world a better place through extensive interaction
with diverse cultures, going beyond observation and participation.
