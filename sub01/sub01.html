Founder’s Message

As the world we live in today is flooded with issues, such as global warming,
international conflict, human rights, and the widening gap between the rich and poor,
there is a greater need to cultivate innovative leaders to set the stage for a new era in
human history.

For the past 25 years, I have devoted myself to overcoming the limits of conventional
educational methods, to finding a groundbreaking method to develop talented
individuals in the global world. In this process, I have traveled all over the world,
benchmarking countless international schools and innovative global school programs.

And after careful thought, I have decided to provide the next generation with a new
'Game Changer' by launching the Next Challenge School.
Next Challenge School hopes that students will self-create their value of life through a
hybrid curriculum that merges exploration with learning. We are proud enough to
claim that it is one of the most challenging and innovative programs in the world.

Do you believe that education means more than merely gaining knowledge?
Do you want to create change while traveling the world?
NCS will help you achieve it. We will help provide an academically rigorous environment, where your children can grow global competitiveness, shaping themselves into a talent that modern society needs.

Founder Cheol Young Kim
