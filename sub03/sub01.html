Admissions Curriculum
Overview
9th & 10,11,12 curriculum
Grade 9

Grade 9 consists of a unique 12-14-12 week trimester system.
In these trimesters, students study in Korea and participate in 8 weeks of global experiences.
In the first trimester, students will visit Japan for 2 weeks as part of a mission trip to contribute to meaningful causes and learn about the challenges that people face on a daily basis.
In the second trimester, 9th graders will gain hands-on experience and spend time working on their projects in Australia for 4 weeks, making the trimester 2 weeks longer than the other two.
In the third trimester, students will be provided with opportunities to collaborate with local communities on the Island of Jeju in Korea.

Grade 10-12

In the first two weeks of each trimester, students will attend an orientation session, and spend the rest of the week planning and preparing for their project(s) through NEXT META. The planning part of the project is as important as the actual implementation. After the first two weeks, students will explore the host country for 10 weeks, taking classes while working towards finishing their NC Capstone project.
Winter and Spring break will be 4 weeks each and Summer break will be 8 weeks long.


For prospective 10th graders, NCS will hold the Global Leadership Camp, where students will participate in 3 weeks of mandatory pre-camp before the official start of the school year. This will serve as the orientation session mentioned above, and students will have 2 more weeks to explore Korea.
