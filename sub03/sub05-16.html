Academic Course Requirements
  • 1. English 4 years
  • 2. Mathematics 4 years
  • 3. Science 3 years (including 2 years of laboratory science)
  • 4. Social Science 3 years (including US History)
  • 5. World Languages 2 years (same language)
  • 6. Bible 4 years (0.5 credit/subject)
  • 7. Electives 3 years (including Health) (0.5 credit/subject)
  • 8. NC Capstone Project 4 years (3.5 credits total)
Capstone Project Requirements

The NC Capstone Project is an opportunity for students to show that they are prepared for college, the contemporary workspace, and becoming contributing members of global society. The capstone program at NCS offers you an opportunity to work on projects following the Design Thinking Process.

As such, students at NCS are required to complete the NC Capstone Program in order to meet graduation requirements.
