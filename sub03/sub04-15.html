Academics Curriculum
Game Changer
Student-led Project

You’ll have the opportunity to pursue one student-led academic project per term under the mentorship of your educator.
In these projects, you’ll form personalized driving questions and choose specific learning targets and 21st-century skills that you want to concentrate on.
This will give you an opportunity to follow your passion and explore areas that spark your interest.
Your personalized projects can be in any form, including taking a few extra AP courses, engaging in scientific research, creating your own art piece, or even just documenting what you have learned about the local culture or history.
Your educator will help you every step of the way, from in-depth designing to objective evaluation of your project. They will also make sure that your project is in accordance with Next Challenge School’s learning targets so that you may receive a well-balanced education that covers a wide range of academic disciplines.

Capstone Project

The Capstone Project is a long-term culminating individual project where students can choose their own topic. At the end of the academic program, students will present their project to the Next Challenge School community, composed of underclassmen, faculty and staff. Capstone projects are not necessarily bound to any specific form. It can be a study on a global issue or phenomenon of your interest, offering possible solutions through a product or a program.
You can conduct scientific research and present its findings through a handmade documentary.
Or you can use any creative way to demonstrate adeptness in a subject.

Academic Portfolio

Throughout the Game Changer Curriculum, there will be voluminous amounts of scholarly output including research papers, media projects, and other forms of documentation of hands-on experiences. This digital collection of output will be your academic portfolio which will exhibit your overall progress.
Towards the end of the program, you will finalize your portfolio, showcasing your works and accomplishments, for your career path.
